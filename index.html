introduction
Hello! I am Seony, an assistant professor at Kyung Hee University, leading Compiler & System Software Laboratory (COS Lab).

I am interested in compiler optimization for performance and programmability, real-time embedded systems, and tiny machine learning. Please visit our lab website (coslab.khu.ac.kr) for more information!
